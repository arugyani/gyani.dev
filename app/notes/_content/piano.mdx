# Assorted piano recordings
The following is an assortment of various performances & recordings I've made of my favorite pieces on the piano in recent years. I've played on and off for most of my life and have found a lot of joy in this instrument.

Some tracks may have background noise due to being directly recorded through a microphone, and others may have poor mixing due to them being recorded through MIDI. I'm working on improving my recording setup & process, so feel free to reach out with any critique!
